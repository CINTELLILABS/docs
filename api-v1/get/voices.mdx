---
title: "List Voice Options"
api: "GET https://api.bland.ai/v1/voices"
description: "Retrieves all available voices for your account."
---

### Headers

<ParamField header="authorization" type="string" required>
  Your API key for authentication.
</ParamField>

### Response

<ResponseField type="array" name="voices">
    Contains a list of the voices available for your account.
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField type="integer" name="voice_id">
    The ID of the voice. Used in conjunction with `reduce_latency` to select the specific voice in a `POST /v1/calls` request.
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField type="string" name="name">
    The name of the voice. This is what will be shown in the dashboard.
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField type="boolean" name="is_custom">
    Whether or not the voice is a custom cloned or uploaded voice tied to your account, or a default voice provided by Bland.
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField type="boolean" name="reduce_latency" default="true">
    Whether the voice is optimized for low latency. Will be `true` for most voices, except for some extra-high quality voices.
</ResponseField>
