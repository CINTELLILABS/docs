### Pick inbound numbers by label when filtering calls

The **To**, **From**, and **Transferred To** filters in call logs now open a searchable list of your inbound numbers, so you can pick a number by its label instead of remembering the digits.

- Focus the **To**, **From**, or **Transferred To** value field in the filter panel to open the picker
- Search by label or by phone number — selecting an entry fills the filter with the matching number
- Free-form phone numbers still work, so existing saved filters and shared filter URLs are unaffected
- Only inbound numbers that have a label set are shown; add or rename labels from your inbound numbers settings to surface them here

See [Call Logs](/tutorials/call-logs) for the full filtering reference.
