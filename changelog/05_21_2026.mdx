### Translation-only audio for warm transfers [Enterprise]

You can now choose whether each listener hears the other speaker's original voice softly under the translated audio during a warm transfer with Live Translation enabled.

- A new **Play the source audio underneath translation** toggle is available inside the Live Translation card on Transfer Call nodes
- **On (default)** preserves today's behavior: each listener hears the other speaker's original voice ducked beneath the translation TTS, so tone, emotion, and pacing carry through
- **Off** delivers clean, translation-only audio on both legs — useful when the underlay feels distracting or when you want the translated voice to be the only thing each party hears
- The toggle only appears after Live Translation is enabled on the node

For full configuration details, see [Warm Transfers → Live Translation](/tutorials/warm-transfer#1-live-translation).
