## Billing Overview

Bland charges based on the actual resources consumed across voice, SMS, and other interaction types. Enterprise accounts may be eligible for custom pricing.

---

## Voice Billing

| Component        | Rate or Rule |
|------------------|--------------|
| **Call Time**    | $0.09 per minute. Prorated to the second. Call initiation (dialing) is excluded. |
| **Transfer Time**| Free if you're using BYOT (Bring Your Own Twilio). <br /> If not, there is a reduced rate of $0.025 per minute. |
| **Voicemail**    | Charged as normal call time. |
| **Failed Calls** | Not billed. |

---

## Warm Transfer Logic

| Scenario                  | Billing Rule |
|---------------------------|--------------|
| **Proxy Agent**           | Charged for talk time while active. |
| **Primary Agent**         | Billing stops once the agent disconnects. |
| **Merged Calls**          | Billed for transfer duration (based on resource ownership). |

---

## SMS Billing

| Type            | Rate         |
|------------------|--------------|
| SMS (in/outbound) | $0.015 per message |

Both incoming and outgoing messages are billed. Pricing is uniform unless otherwise agreed upon in an enterprise contract.
